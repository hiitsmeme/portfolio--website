Reading

For as long as I can think, I have liked reading. But only since my mother bought me the complete Harry Potter series for my birthday I read a lot. I try to read in as many genres as possible to widen my horizon, even though my favourite genre is fantasy.

1Q84-Haruki Murakami

This book is really bizarre. Aomame, a professional serial killer, comes because of multiple events to the conclusion that she is in a parallel universe which she calls 1Q84. Tengo is a math teacher and hobby-author. Throughout this book their fates intertwine.

In Other Lands-Sarah Rees Brennan

In Other Lands is my favourite fantasy book. Elliot, the main character, gets to choose if he wants to live in a land called The Other Lands beyond an invisible wall. As he doesn't have any friends or important family, he leaves. Behind the wall he finds a land where many tribes including mermaids are at war. Thus he decides bring the wars to an end using only his wits and sarcasm.

Sprache und Sein-Kübra Gümüsay

In this book the author talks about the sexism and racism ingrained into our languages and thus our thinking. She discusses the racism she had to face throughout her life. For me personally, this book was eye-opening.
